💡 Expert Tips

Score High in CBSE Class 11 — 2026-27

Class 11 is the hardest jump in your school career. These tips will help you stay ahead.

🎯
Class 11 = 45% of JEE/NEET
JEE and NEET have 40-45% questions from Class 11. Mechanics (Physics), Organic Chemistry, Calculus (Maths), and Human Physiology (Biology) are highest-weighted. Build strong foundations now.
⚡
Physics: Master NLM Early
Laws of Motion (NLM) is the most important chapter for both boards and JEE. It connects to Work-Energy, Rotational Motion, and Gravitation. Don't move forward without mastering NLM.
🧪
Chemistry: NCERT is Everything
For Inorganic and Physical Chemistry, NCERT is sufficient for school exams. For Organic Chemistry, understand mechanisms deeply — don't memorise reactions without understanding why.
📐
Maths: Calculus Changes Everything
Limits and Derivatives introduce calculus — a completely new concept. If you master this in Class 11, integration in Class 12 becomes easy. Practice 10 problems daily on derivatives.
🌱
Biology: Draw Diagrams Daily
NEET Biology needs neat, well-labelled diagrams. Practice drawing cell organelles, plant anatomy, animal kingdom diagrams daily. Biomolecules and Human Physiology are highest-scoring for NEET.
💼
Commerce: Accounts = Practice Daily
Accountancy is a scoring subject but only if you practice daily. Financial statements and BRS need regular problem-solving. Aim for 90%+ in Accounts to boost your overall Commerce score.
