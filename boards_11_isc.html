ISC Class 11 — All Streams 2026-27

Official CISCE 2026-27 syllabus from cisce.org. ISC is more detailed and analytical than CBSE. Select your stream and subject.

Significant figures, SI units, laws of chemical combination, Dalton's theory, mole concept, empirical and molecular formula, stoichiometry, limiting reagent calculations

Important

Chemical Bonding
Ionic, covalent, coordinate bonds; Lewis structures; VSEPR theory; hybridisation (sp, sp2, sp3, sp3d, sp3d2); MOT (H2, N2, O2, F2, CO, NO); dipole moment; H-bonding types
Important
Ch.4
Periodic Table and Periodicity
Mendeleev's and modern periodic tables, s/p/d/f blocks, periodic trends (atomic radius, ionisation energy, electron affinity, electronegativity, metallic character) with explanation
Important
Ch.5
Chemical Thermodynamics
System and surroundings, enthalpy (ΔH), Hess's law, bond enthalpies, entropy (ΔS), Gibbs free energy (ΔG), spontaneity conditions, equilibrium condition ΔG=0
Important
Ch.6
Chemical Equilibrium
Law of mass action, Kc and Kp relation, Le Chatelier's principle, ionic equilibrium, pH calculations (strong/weak acid/base), buffer solutions, solubility product (Ksp), common ion effect
Important

Hydrogen
Position in periodic table, isotopes, preparation (Bosch process, electrolysis), physical and chemical properties, reducing nature, uses, heavy water, hydrogen as fuel

Group 1 (alkali metals) and Group 2 (alkaline earth metals) — general characteristics, anomalous properties of Li and Be, compounds (NaOH, Na2CO3, Na2SO4), biological importance

Ch.9

IUPAC nomenclature (alkanes, alkenes, alkynes, functional groups), isomerism (structural, stereo), electronic effects (inductive, resonance, hyperconjugation), reaction intermediates
Important

💡 Expert Tips

Score High in ISC Class 11 — 2026-27

ISC demands deeper understanding and detailed answers. These tips are specific to the CISCE ISC pattern.

📚
ISC is Deeper than CBSE
ISC Maths has Harmonic Progressions, De Moivre's theorem, and detailed conic sections not in CBSE. ISC Chemistry covers Environmental Chemistry and s-Block separately. Plan more time per chapter.
📝
Write Long Answers Well
ISC values detailed, structured answers. Use headings, sub-points, and diagrams. A 5-mark answer needs 5 clear points. Practice writing answers within word limits from Day 1.
🧪
Chemistry: Mechanisms are Key
ISC Organic Chemistry requires full mechanism writing (arrow pushing). SN1, SN2, electrophilic addition, EAS — you must draw these correctly for full marks. CBSE doesn't test mechanisms as deeply.
📐
Maths: Section A is Compulsory
ISC Maths has Section A (compulsory), Section B (Pure Maths), and Section C (Applied/Statistics). Focus on Section A first — it covers the most marks. Don't attempt Section B before mastering A.
📖
English: Literature is Half the Paper
ISC English Literature (Treasure Trove + prescribed novel/play) carries 40+ marks. Read every prescribed text at least twice. Learn character names, themes, and key quotes for each piece.
🌱
Biology: Diagrams = Marks
ISC Biology is heavy on detailed diagrams. Meiosis stages, chloroplast structure, nephron, heart, motor neuron — practice drawing and labelling these without reference. Neat diagrams earn full marks.
