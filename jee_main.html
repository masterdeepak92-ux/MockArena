Preparation Strategy
📅
Month 1–2: Foundation
Cover NCERT thoroughly. Focus on Class 11 topics — Mechanics, Thermodynamics and Organic Chemistry basics.
🔢
Month 3–4: Class 12
Electrostatics, EMI, Electrochemistry, Coordination Compounds, Calculus, 3D Geometry.
📝
Month 5–6: Practice
Solve 50+ questions daily. Take chapter tests weekly. Identify weak areas and revise.
🎯
Final Month: Mocks
Take full 3-hour mock tests every 2 days. Analyse mistakes. Focus on time management.
⚡
High Weightage Topics
Calculus, Electrostatics, Organic Chemistry, Coordinate Geometry, Thermodynamics — these appear every year.
🚫
Removed Topics
Don't waste time on Capacitors, Communication Systems, Environmental Chemistry, States of Matter, Mathematical Reasoning.
