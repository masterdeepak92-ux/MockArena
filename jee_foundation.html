Why Start in Class 8?

Class 8–10 is when your brain forms the strongest conceptual connections. Build correct mental models now — no cramming later.

📈

Study Strategy

How to Study Smart in Class 8–10

Strategies from students who cracked IIT-JEE starting their prep from Class 8.

1
NCERT is Your Bible
Read every word — theory, examples, exercises. NCERT mastery in Class 9-10 means JEE concepts feel familiar in Class 11.
2
Understand, Don't Memorise
Ask "Why?" after every formula. If you understand why F = ma, you'll never forget it and can solve any variant.
3
Daily Problem Practice
30 minutes daily for 3 years beats 3 months of cramming. Solve at least 10 problems daily — consistency is everything.
4
Try Harder Problems
After mastering NCERT, try JEE-level problems on MockArena. The struggle is how you grow — don't be afraid to attempt hard questions.
5
Appear for Olympiads
NSO, NMTC, NTSE — these push your thinking beyond school and are great for confidence, scholarships and college applications.
6
Track Your Mistakes
Maintain an error notebook. Every wrong answer shows exactly what to fix. Review before every test — this is how toppers improve fast.
