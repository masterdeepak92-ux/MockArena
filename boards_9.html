Study Strategy

How to Score 90%+ in Class 9

Strategies that work — from students who aced Class 9 and went on to top boards, JEE and NEET ranks.

1
NCERT is Everything
For CBSE Class 9, NCERT textbooks are the exam. Read every example, every exercise question. 90%+ of exam questions come directly from NCERT.
2
Maths: Solve Daily
Maths is not a reading subject — it is a solving subject. Do minimum 10 problems daily. Every chapter needs practice, not just understanding.
3
Science: Diagrams are Marks
Draw Cell diagrams, plant & animal tissue diagrams, motion graphs weekly from memory. Diagrams are asked directly in exams for 2-3 marks each.
4
Don't Skip SST
Students who study SST seriously in Class 9 find Class 10 SST boards very easy. 30 minutes daily of SST reading is enough — don't ignore it.
5
Take Tests Monthly
Use MockArena Physics tests monthly to check your understanding. Wrong answers teach more than right ones — review them the next morning carefully.
6
Build for the Future
Study Class 9 Physics with JEE in mind. Study Biology with NEET in mind. This mindset creates 3x deeper understanding than just "studying for marks".
