Board Strategy

How to Score 95%+ in Class 10 Boards

Proven strategies from students who scored 95%+ in CBSE Class 10 boards and went on to top JEE/NEET ranks.

1
NCERT is the Exam
For CBSE boards, NCERT textbooks ARE the exam. Read every example, every exercise, every intext question. 95% of board questions come directly from NCERT.
2
Biology — Every Line, Every Diagram
Class 10 Biology carries the most marks per chapter. Draw Life Processes, Reproduction, Nervous System diagrams from memory weekly — they appear directly in boards.
3
Math — Solve, Not Just Read
Solve every NCERT exercise. Then solve previous year board papers. Math needs 30+ problems solved daily — understanding without practice loses marks.
4
Physics — Formulae + Numericals
Electricity and Light chapters have both theory and numericals. Solve every NCERT numerical twice. Board papers always have 2-3 numerical questions per chapter.
5
SST + English = Easy 95
SST and English are the easiest subjects to score 95%+. 30 min daily of SST reading + 1 writing practice per week in English gives near-full marks.
6
Solve 5 Years of PYQ
Last 5 years of CBSE Class 10 board papers. Exam setters follow patterns — same question types repeat. Solving PYQ trains exam pattern recognition dramatically.
