💡 Expert Tips

Score 90+ in State Board Class 10

Proven strategies from students who scored 90%+ in state board exams.

📝
Write Neatly & Structure Answers
CBSE awards step marks. Even if your final answer is wrong, neat steps with correct method get you partial marks. Use diagrams wherever possible.
⏱️
Manage Time in the Exam Hall
Spend 1.5 minutes per mark. For a 3-mark question — 4.5 minutes max. Don't spend 20 minutes on a question you're stuck on. Move on and come back.
📋
Read the Paper First — 15 Minutes
CBSE gives 15 minutes reading time before writing. Use every second. Plan which questions to attempt first and which optional questions to choose.
🔄
Revise Using PYQ Solutions
CBSE often repeats similar question types. Study the official marking scheme — it shows exactly what points to write for full marks.
