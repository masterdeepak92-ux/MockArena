Common Mistakes

Top NEET Mistakes to Avoid

These are the exact mistakes that cost students 50-100 marks in NEET. Know them. Avoid them. They are the difference between a seat and no seat.

MISTAKE 01
Skipping NCERT for Coaching Notes
90% of NEET Biology is directly from NCERT. Students who rely only on coaching notes miss exact NCERT phrasing that NEET uses in questions.
Fix: Read NCERT first, coaching notes second — always.
MISTAKE 02
Neglecting Physics Completely
Many students spend 80% of time on Biology and ignore Physics. Then Physics pulls their rank from top 5000 to top 50,000 single-handedly.
Fix: Minimum 2 hours Physics daily, even during Biology-heavy phases.
MISTAKE 03
Guessing Randomly on MCQs
Random guessing with −1 penalty is statistically guaranteed to lose marks. 4 options, −1 wrong, +4 correct = expected value of random guess is −0.25 per question.
Fix: Only attempt if 70%+ confident. Skip otherwise — 0 is better than −1.
MISTAKE 04
No Full Mock Practice
Students practice chapters but never simulate a full 3h 20min NEET. On exam day, they mentally fatigue in hour 2 — Biology answers become careless.
Fix: Full mock every 2 weeks from 6 months before exam.
MISTAKE 05
Ignoring Diagrams in Biology
NEET asks 15-20 diagram-based questions every year — mitosis, meiosis, heart, kidney, neuron, flower. Students who skip diagrams lose 60-80 marks instantly.
Fix: Draw every NCERT diagram from memory weekly. No shortcuts.
MISTAKE 06
Not Reviewing Wrong Answers
Taking mocks without reviewing wrong answers is the single biggest waste of preparation time. The lesson is in the wrong answer — not the correct one.
Fix: Spend equal time reviewing wrong answers as taking the test itself.

NEET Strategy

How to Crack NEET UG

Proven strategies from students who scored 650+ and secured AIIMS or top government MBBS seats on first attempt.

1
NCERT = 90% of NEET Biology
Read every line, every diagram, every intext question — minimum 5-6 revisions. NCERT mastery alone gives 300+ marks. There are absolutely zero shortcuts here.
2
70%+ Confident = Always Attempt
Educated elimination gives positive expected marks. The −1 penalty only hurts pure random guessing. Think 15 seconds — if 70%+ confident, mark and move.
3
Biology First in Every Mock
You are freshest at the start. Biology = 50% marks, tests recall not calculation. Save Physics for peak focus. Never start with Physics — you will waste freshness.
4
Physics Decides Your Final Rank
Top scorers all score similarly in Bio and Chem. Physics separates AIR 100 from AIR 10,000. Complete all 13 Physics chapter tests on MockArena — every single one.
5
10 Years of PYQ is Non-Negotiable
NEET repeats concept patterns every single year. 10 years of PYQ trains deep pattern recognition — you will see familiar question types on exam day and answer faster.
6
Full Mock Every 2 Weeks
Full 3h 20min NEET mock every 2 weeks minimum. Review every wrong answer the next morning. Your accuracy must improve with every mock. Track it — or it won't.
