Preparation Strategy
📖
NCERT is Bible
95% of NEET questions come directly from NCERT lines and diagrams. Read every line of Class 11 & 12 NCERT multiple times.
🌿
Biology = 50% Marks
Biology has 90 questions (360 marks). Master it first. Genetics, Ecology, Photosynthesis and Human Physiology are highest scoring.
🔬
Diagrams are Critical
Learn to label all NCERT diagrams — heart, nephron, brain, mitochondria, cell, life cycles. Several questions come from diagrams directly.
⚡
Physics is Formula Based
NEET Physics is easier than JEE. Focus on Mechanics, Electrostatics, Modern Physics and Optics. Formula application is key.
🧪
Chemistry from NCERT Only
Organic reactions, p-Block elements, Coordination Compounds — stick to NCERT only. Don't use reference books for NEET Chemistry.
🎯
Target 550+ in Mocks
Take full 180-question mock every week. Aim for 550+ consistently before the real exam. Analyse each mistake carefully.
