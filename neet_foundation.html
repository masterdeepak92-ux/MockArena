Study Strategy

How to Study Smart in Class 8–10

Strategies from students who cracked NEET with top ranks — starting their preparation from Class 8.

1
NCERT Biology = NEET Bible
90% of NEET Biology is directly from NCERT. Read every line, every diagram, every example box. Revise minimum 5 times. There are no shortcuts.
2
Draw Diagrams Regularly
Biology is visual. Draw Cell structure, Flower, Human Heart, Digestive system from memory every week. NEET has 10-15 direct diagram marks.
3
Make a Biology Notebook
Write every fact, exception and scientific name. Your Class 8-10 notes become the most valuable revision material in your Class 12 final sprint.
4
Solve NCERT Questions Daily
Many NEET questions are direct or slightly modified NCERT questions. Solve every in-text and end-of-chapter question. Don't skip a single one.
5
Build Physics & Chemistry Too
Biology is 50% but Physics (25%) and Chemistry (25%) decide your rank among top scorers. Build all 3 subjects simultaneously from Class 8 itself.
6
Take Mock Tests Monthly
Use MockArena NEET mock monthly to assess your level. Reviewing wrong answers teaches 10x more than reading correct ones. Track your progress.
