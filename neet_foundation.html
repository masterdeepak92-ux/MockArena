Why Start in Class 9–10?
⏳
2 Extra Years
Starting in Class 9 gives you 2 extra years compared to students who begin in Class 11 — a massive head start for NEET.
🧠
Stronger Fundamentals
Class 11–12 Biology, Physics and Chemistry build directly on Class 9–10 concepts. Weak basics make NEET-level topics much harder.
📊
Better Board Results
The conceptual clarity from NEET-style practice directly improves Class 9–10 board exam performance too — dual benefit.
🎯
Reduced Class 11–12 Load
With a strong foundation, Class 11–12 becomes about depth and NCERT mastery rather than learning concepts from scratch under pressure.
🔬
Biology Head Start
NEET Biology is 50% of the exam and entirely NCERT-based. Early exposure to cell biology, genetics basics and plant/animal systems pays off hugely.
📈
Confidence Building
Solving NEET-pattern questions early builds confidence and removes exam fear when you reach Class 11–12.
