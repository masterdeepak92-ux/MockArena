🏆

🩺

Class 12 Syllabus

What You Study in Class 12

CBSE Class 12 Science — the chapters that complete the JEE and NEET syllabus. Class 12 provides the remaining 40-50%.

⚡
Electrostatics & Magnetism
Units 1-5 · Very High JEE weightage
Electric Charges & Fields — Coulomb, field lines, flux
Electrostatic Potential & Capacitance — Energy, dielectrics
Current Electricity — Ohm's law, Kirchhoff's laws, Wheatstone
Moving Charges & Magnetism — Lorentz force, Biot-Savart
Magnetism & Matter — Dia, para, ferromagnetism, Earth's field
Electromagnetic Induction — Faraday, Lenz, eddy currents
Alternating Current — RLC, resonance, transformers, power
🎯 EMI + AC + Electrostatics = 25-30% of JEE Physics. These are Class 12 chapters.
🔭
Optics & Modern Physics
Units 6-10 · JEE + NEET both
Electromagnetic Waves — EM spectrum, properties
Ray Optics — Mirrors, lenses, optical instruments, refraction
Wave Optics — Huygens, interference, diffraction, polarisation
Dual Nature — Photoelectric effect, de Broglie, matter waves
Atoms & Nuclei — Bohr model, nuclear reactions, radioactivity
Semiconductor Electronics — p-n junction, transistor, logic gates
🎯 Optics + Modern Physics = 20%+ JEE Physics. Also high NEET weightage.
🧪
Physical & Inorganic Chemistry
Highest JEE + NEET weightage
Solutions — Raoult's law, colligative properties, osmosis
Electrochemistry — Nernst equation, galvanic cells, electrolysis
Chemical Kinetics — Rate laws, Arrhenius, order of reaction
d-Block & f-Block Elements — Transition metals, lanthanides
Coordination Chemistry — IUPAC, VBT, CFT, isomerism
Haloalkanes & Haloarenes — Nucleophilic substitution reactions
🎯 Coordination Chemistry is the hardest Class 12 Chem chapter. Start early.
🔬
Organic Chemistry
Very High JEE + NEET weightage
Alcohols, Phenols & Ethers — Reactions, acidic character
Aldehydes, Ketones & Carboxylic Acids — Nucleophilic addition
Amines — Classification, preparation, reactions, diazonium
Biomolecules — Carbohydrates, proteins, nucleic acids, enzymes
Polymers — Classification, addition, condensation polymers
Chemistry in Everyday Life — Drugs, soaps, food preservatives
🎯 Organic Chemistry Class 12 = highest Chemistry weightage in both JEE and NEET.
∫
Calculus — JEE Core
Highest JEE Math weightage
Relations & Functions — Types, composition, inverse
Inverse Trigonometry — Principal values, properties, equations
Matrices & Determinants — Operations, inverse, Cramer's rule
Continuity & Differentiability — Derivative rules, parametric
Applications of Derivatives — Maxima, minima, tangents
Integrals — Indefinite, definite, properties, ILATE rule
Applications of Integrals — Area between curves
Differential Equations — Formation, variable separable, linear
🎯 Integration alone is 20%+ of JEE Mains Math. Master it completely.
📐
Algebra, Vectors & 3D
High JEE weightage
Vectors — Dot product, cross product, scalar triple product
3D Geometry — Lines, planes, distance, angle between planes
Linear Programming — Graphical method, feasible region
Probability — Conditional probability, Bayes theorem, Bernoulli
⚡ Vectors + 3D Geometry = 15%+ JEE Math. Straight-forward marks — don't skip.
🧬
Reproduction & Genetics
Very High NEET — 50+ marks
Reproduction in Organisms — Asexual, sexual, types
Sexual Reproduction in Flowering Plants — Pollination, fertilisation
Human Reproduction — Gametogenesis, fertilisation, development
Reproductive Health — Contraception, STDs, infertility
Principles of Inheritance & Variation — Mendel, deviations
Molecular Basis of Inheritance — DNA structure, replication, transcription
Evolution — Origin of life, Darwinism, evidence, speciation
🔥 Genetics + Molecular Biology = highest NEET Class 12 marks. Every line is a question.
🌿
Biology in Human Welfare & Ecology
High NEET — 40-50 marks
Human Health & Disease — Pathogens, immunity, AIDS, cancer
Strategies for Enhancement in Food Production — Plant breeding
Microbes in Human Welfare — Biogas, sewage, antibiotics
Biotechnology — Principles, DNA fingerprinting, cloning
Biotechnology & Its Applications — GMOs, insulin, gene therapy
Organisms & Populations — Ecology, population interactions
Ecosystem — Energy flow, nutrient cycles, succession, services
Biodiversity & Conservation — Hotspots, threats, conservation
🔥 Ecology + Biodiversity = 10-15 NEET marks every year. Easy marks — don't neglect.

Year Strategy

How to Crack Class 12 — Boards + JEE/NEET

The strategy that lets you score 95% in boards AND get a top JEE/NEET rank at the same time.

1
Boards + Competitive = Same Prep
Class 12 syllabus is identical for boards and JEE/NEET. One deep preparation serves both. Don't study separately for boards and JEE — it doubles your effort for no gain.
2
JEE Mains Session 1 in January
Give JEE Session 1 in January before boards. It tells you exactly where you stand. Even a moderate score gives you confidence and your best attempt is April — after boards.
3
NCERT for Boards + NEET Bio
For board exams: NCERT is the paper. For NEET Biology: NCERT is the exam. Read every line 5-6 times. This habit started in Class 12 itself is powerful enough.
4
Electrostatics + Optics First
These are the two highest-weightage Class 12 Physics chapters for both JEE and NEET. Master them completely before anything else. Start MockArena chapter tests immediately.
5
Monthly Full Mock — Mandatory
Give a full JEE Mains or NEET mock every month from August of Class 12. Time yourself. Review wrong answers. Your score must improve every month — track it obsessively.
6
Last 3 Months = Pure Revision
No new topics after January. Full syllabus revision + PYQ practice only. Students who chase new topics in February lose their existing strong chapters. Consolidate — don't expand.
