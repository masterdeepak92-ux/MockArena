Class 11 Syllabus

What You Study in Class 11

CBSE Class 11 Science — the chapters that form 50-60% of JEE Mains and NEET question papers.

⚛️
Mechanics
Units 1-5 · Highest JEE weightage
Units & Dimensions — Errors, significant figures
Kinematics — Vectors, projectile, relative motion
Laws of Motion — Friction, circular motion, NLM
Work, Energy, Power — Conservation laws
Rotational Motion — Moment of inertia, torque, rolling
Gravitation — Orbital motion, escape velocity, satellites
🎯 50%+ of JEE Physics comes from Mechanics. Master this first.
🌡️
Thermodynamics & Waves
Units 6-10
Properties of Matter — Elasticity, surface tension, viscosity
Thermodynamics — Laws, heat engines, entropy
Kinetic Theory of Gases — PV=nRT, degrees of freedom
Oscillations — SHM, energy, damped oscillations
Waves — Speed, superposition, standing waves, Doppler
⚡ Thermodynamics + Waves appear in both JEE and NEET. Study once, score twice.
🧪
Physical Chemistry
Numerical + Conceptual
Mole Concept & Stoichiometry — Calculations, limiting reagent
Atomic Structure — Quantum numbers, orbitals, electronic config
Chemical Bonding & MOT — VSEPR, hybridisation, MOT
States of Matter — Gas laws, liquid state, solutions
Thermodynamics — Enthalpy, Hess's law, spontaneity
Equilibrium — Le Chatelier, Kp, Kc, ionic equilibrium
🎯 Physical Chem needs daily problem solving — not just concept reading.
🔬
Organic & Inorganic
GOC + p-block
Organic Chemistry GOC — Inductive, resonance, hyperconjugation
Hydrocarbons — Alkanes, alkenes, alkynes, reactions
Periodic Table & Properties — Trends, ionisation energy
Hydrogen — Properties, preparation, water chemistry
s-Block Elements — Group 1 & 2 compounds, reactions
p-Block — Group 13 & 14 elements, compounds
🎯 GOC + Hydrocarbons = highest Organic Chemistry weightage in both JEE and NEET.
📐
Algebra & Trigonometry
JEE core units
Sets, Relations & Functions — Domain, range, types
Trigonometry — All formulae, inverse trig, equations
Complex Numbers — Argand plane, De Moivre, roots
Quadratic Equations — Nature of roots, inequalities
Sequences & Series — AP, GP, AGP, sum to infinity
Permutations & Combinations — nCr, counting principles
Binomial Theorem — General term, middle term
🎯 Trigonometry + Complex Numbers = 20%+ of JEE Maths. Master them early.
∫
Coordinate & Calculus
New in Class 11
Straight Lines — All forms, angle between lines, distance
Conic Sections — Circle, parabola, ellipse, hyperbola
3D Geometry Introduction — Direction cosines, lines
Limits & Derivatives — First principle, standard results
Mathematical Reasoning — Statements, connectives
Statistics — Mean, variance, standard deviation
Probability — Events, Bayes theorem
🎯 Limits & Derivatives introduction here — Integration comes in Class 12. Don't skip.
🧬
Diversity & Structural Organisation
NEET core — 40-50 marks
The Living World — Characteristics, taxonomy, nomenclature
Biological Classification — 5 kingdoms, viruses, lichens
Plant Kingdom — Algae to Angiosperms, alternation of generations
Animal Kingdom — Classification, phyla characteristics
Morphology of Flowering Plants — Root, stem, leaf, flower
Anatomy of Flowering Plants — Tissues, tissue systems
Structural Organisation in Animals — Tissues, earthworm, cockroach
🔥 Plant + Animal Kingdom = highest NEET Biology weightage. Every line = marks.
🔬
Cell Biology & Physiology
NEET core — 50-60 marks
Cell — The Unit of Life: prokaryotic, eukaryotic, organelles
Biomolecules — Carbohydrates, proteins, lipids, nucleic acids
Cell Cycle & Cell Division — Mitosis, meiosis in detail
Photosynthesis — Light & dark reaction, C3/C4, photorespiration
Respiration in Plants — Glycolysis, Krebs, ETC, fermentation
Plant Growth & Development — Hormones, photoperiodism
Digestion & Absorption, Breathing & Exchange of Gases
🔥 Cell + Biomolecules + Photosynthesis — read NCERT 4-5 times. Every detail matters.

Study Strategy

How to Ace Class 11 Science

From students who topped Class 11, cracked JEE and NEET simultaneously.

1
NCERT First — Always
For boards AND competitive exams, NCERT is the backbone. Read every chapter thoroughly before coaching material. Concept clarity from NCERT saves hours later.
2
Don't Fall Behind
Class 11 pace is brutal. If you miss one week, it takes a month to recover. Stay current — every single day. Daily study is more effective than weekend cramming.
3
Physics — Concept + Problems
Physics is not a reading subject. After understanding the concept, solve 20 problems minimum per chapter. Rotational Motion and Thermodynamics need 50+ problems each.
4
Chemistry — 3 Different Approaches
Physical = solve daily. Organic = understand mechanisms. Inorganic = read + revise. Treating all three the same is the biggest Chemistry mistake in Class 11.
5
PCB: Biology is 50% of NEET
If you are a NEET aspirant, Biology is your priority. NCERT Biology read 4-5 times is worth more than any coaching notes. Start this habit in Class 11 itself.
6
Take Monthly Mock Tests
Give JEE or NEET level mock tests monthly from Class 11 itself. It shows you exactly where you stand and builds exam stamina much before Class 12 pressure begins.
