    FAQ

    Questions? Answered.

    Is MockArena suitable for Class 9 and 10 students?
    +
    Yes! MockArena has dedicated Foundation programs for Class 9 and 10 students preparing for JEE and NEET. The content is aligned with CBSE curriculum while introducing competitive exam concepts early, giving students a significant head start.
    How is MockArena different from other test prep platforms?
    +
    MockArena is built around deep analytics and competitive ranking, not just test delivery. After every test, you get question-level time analysis, chapter-wise accuracy, AIR prediction, and a clear action plan. Most platforms show you scores; we show you exactly how to improve them.
    Are the test questions original or copied from other sources?
    +
    Our question bank includes a mix of original questions crafted by IIT alumni and experienced faculty, previous year questions from JEE and NEET (with detailed solutions), and curated practice questions. Every question is reviewed for accuracy and exam relevance.
    Can I use MockArena on my phone?
    +
    Absolutely. MockArena is available on web browsers and as dedicated iOS and Android apps. Your progress syncs across all devices seamlessly, so you can study on the go and take tests on a full screen when needed.
    What happens after my 7-day free trial?
    +
    After the 7-day trial, you can choose the Explorer (free) plan or upgrade to Scholar or Elite. You won't be charged automatically — we'll ask you to choose a plan. Your test history and analytics are preserved regardless of the plan you choose.
    Does MockArena cover CBSE boards alongside competitive prep?
    +
    Yes. We understand that Class 11 and 12 students need to simultaneously prepare for boards and competitive exams. MockArena offers CBSE-pattern chapter tests, sample papers, and a dedicated board module that runs alongside your JEE or NEET preparation.
